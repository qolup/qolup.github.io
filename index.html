In Breathsleep, we provide comfort and peace

Obstructive Sleep Apnea (OSA) is an sleep disorder that mainly caused by collapse of upper airway due to muscle relaxation in rapid-eye-movement sleep, which ultimately lead to frequent arousal and fragmentation of sleep1,2. The overall prevalence of OSA in Korea is 27% in male and 16% in female respectively while prevalence of high risk of OSA is 15.8% male predominance demonstrate its significance2,3.

Prolong untreated OSA would cause high blood pressure, diabetes mellitus, chronic fatigue, headache, weight gain and work inefficiency4. Current treatments for OSA, surgical treatment or Continuous Positive Airway Pressure (CPAP), do not provide a definite cure while possess a risk of invasiveness or inconveniences in long-term usage2,3. Ultimately lead to negligence in treatment of OSA.

We, Breathsleep, believe initial treatment of OSA starts from bedside life-style modification.

Portable motion sensor senses the chest movement and constantly interacts with LED and vibrator-adapted eye-mask. Just a simple 10 seconds of stationary state initiate a gentle light arousal and spontaneous body reposition. Sleep characteristics are consistently monitored, analyzed to be personalized.
