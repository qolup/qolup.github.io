We, Breathsleep, will

Free you from imperceptible stuffiness

It is difficult to hold your breath just for a simple 30 seconds. With underlying OSA, some would not breathe for more than 1 minute. Repeated oxygen deprivation of our body would increase more stress and gradually impair your health. Breathsleep assists you to break this cycle and free you from imperceptible stuffiness in your sleep.

Free you from anxiety

Unlike other devices which only monitor the sleeping pattern, Breathsleep not only monitor your sleeping characteristics but also analyze and personalize it to provide optimal sleeping environment. Self-checking of sleeping pattern and visualizing slow yet gradual progress would break the vicious cycle of anxiety of sleep apnea. Just put on the eye-mask and free yourself out from this busy world with a short nap or with a good-night sleep.

Free you from inconvenience & Accompany you to better sleep

Inconvenience of CPAP mask for its hoses, straps, noises and hygiene are difficult to sustain for long-term usage. While prolonged use to CPAP would lead to nasal or oral dryness5. Our Breathsleep eye-mask block out all the possible light sources and provide your with more optimal sleeping environment in daytime.
